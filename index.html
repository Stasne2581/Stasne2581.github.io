What is it ?

Super Mario & Donkey Kong: Spark Destiny is an ambitious fan game that brings Mario and Donkey Kong together for a brand-new, explosive crossover adventure. Team up, explore, and overcome obstacles in a quest built by fans, for fans!

Features: Switch Character System, Multiplayer Mode, 3D World Map, Original Enemies, Custom Soundtracks, and much more!

The Story

Peace never lasts long in the Mushroom Kingdom. In a desperate bid for ultimate power, Bowser has forged an unexpected alliance with the cruel King K. Rool! Together, they have stolen the legendary Grand Spark, an ancient artifact with great power.

To stop this chaotic duo and restore balance, Mario and Donkey Kong must unite their strengths. Embark on an epic journey through 7 original, dangerous worlds, solve clever puzzles, and defeat the combined armies of the Koopas and the Kremlings before time runs out!
