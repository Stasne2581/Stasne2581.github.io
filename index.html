What is it ?

Super Mario And Donkey Kong Spark's Destiny is a FAN GAME that introduce a new adventure with Mario and Donkey Kong. Live your adventure trough 7 original worlds where you will stop Bowser and King K.Rool from stealing the grand spark.

Features: Switch Character System, Multiplayer, 3D World Map, Original Enemies and more !
