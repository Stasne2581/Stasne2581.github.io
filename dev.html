Development

Take a look behind the scenes of the creation of Super Mario & DK. This project was born from a passion to offer a unique crossover, combining the best of both universes.

The Game Engine

The game is fully developed using GameMaker. This choice allows us to design precise, responsive 2D platforming gameplay that is perfectly optimized for different platforms (Windows, Android, and Nintendo Switch).

Technical Challenges

  • The Switch System: Optimizaing so that the switch can handle the game without any lag
  • Multiplayer Mode: Synchronizing player actions to guarantee a fun, lag-free local co-op experience.
  • Cross-Platform Optimization: Adapting the controls for touch screens on Android, controllers on Switch, and keyboard/mouse on PC.

Tools Used

  • Code & Logic: GameMaker (GML)
  • Graphics & Pixel Art: Pix2D
  • Music & SFX: FL Studio

Current Status & Goals

The mario mouvements are stable even tho there's still some issues. We are currently focusing on level design, map design, and Donkey Kong movements and sprites.
